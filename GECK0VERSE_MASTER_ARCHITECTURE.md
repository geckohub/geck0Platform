Version: 1.0
Status: Living Document

=============================================================
MISSION
=============================================================

Geck0verse is a modular, AI-first operating platform that
unifies cybersecurity, automation, AI, knowledge management,
digital creation, infrastructure management and personal life
into one integrated ecosystem.

Every application should contribute to the same intelligence
layer.

Every module should be searchable.

Every interaction should improve the system.
