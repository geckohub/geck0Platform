# BreadCrumbs Development Notes

## Vision

BreadCrumbs is the AI digital product creation platform under Pink Geck0.

It should be able to take requirements from text, images, files, emails, web forms, Discord, Telegram, WhatsApp, secure copy, or Crumble, then create digital products.

## Product types

- websites
- campaign pages
- dashboards
- apps
- scripts
- databases
- reports
- videos
- narrated documentaries
- Alternative Timelines content

## Relationship with Geck0 Earth

Geck0 Earth generates story data:
- map camera path
- timeline events
- layer toggles
- narration script
- image/video references
- citations/source notes

BreadCrumbs converts that into:
- video script
- storyboard
- generated scenes
- narration audio
- map animation sequence
- final video package

## Alternative Timelines

A creative YouTube/content arm for speculative history and mockumentaries.

Examples:
- What if dinosaurs survived?
- What if Rome never collapsed?
- What if Germany won WWII?
- What if London became a rainforest?
- What if Britain had a giant tsunami?
