# Geck0 Air and Geck0 Radio

## Geck0 Air

Geck0 Air is the RF/environmental sensor intelligence platform.

Scope:
- Wi-Fi
- Bluetooth
- WiGLE imports
- ESP32 sensors
- Pineapple data
- Flipper observations
- local signal mapping
- RF heatmaps
- environmental sensors

Integration with Geck0 Earth:
- local signal overlays
- wardriving maps
- Wi-Fi/Bluetooth density
- time-based signal replay
- device/sensor history

## Geck0 Radio

Geck0 Radio focuses on audio/visual signals and radio spectrum.

Scope:
- FM/AM
- DAB
- SDR waterfalls
- shortwave
- ham radio
- marine radio
- aviation radio
- CB
- TV signals where available

Integration with Geck0 Earth:
- transmitter maps
- propagation layers
- live radio stations by area
- local broadcast discovery
- signal/time overlays
